Course Syllabus

What You Will Learn

Learn to create professional websites using WordPress. This module covers themes, plugins, layouts, and responsiveness. You’ll build blogs, landing pages, and business sites.

Explore AI tools for keyword research, competitor analysis, and ranking. Learn on-page, off-page SEO, link building, and generate audit reports to boost visibility.

Generate blogs, descriptions, captions, ad copies, and website content using AI. Learn to optimize for SEO, engage audiences, and adapt tone across platforms.

Use AI tools to create professional videos, avatars, and voiceovers. From short reels to explainers, learn AI-driven video creation for storytelling and engagement.

Use AI tools for recording, editing, and enhancing podcasts. Learn strategies to distribute on Spotify, YouTube, Apple Podcasts, and promote effectively.

Optimize ad campaigns on Google, Facebook, and Instagram with AI. Learn audience targeting, A/B testing, and budget optimization for maximum ROI.

AI helps create personalized campaigns, optimize subject lines, and write engaging content. Track open rates, clicks, and conversions.

Cover GA4, dashboards, and predictive analytics. Learn to forecast buying patterns, analyze customer journeys, and make smarter decisions with data.

Turn your skills into income. Learn freelancing essentials: profile building, writing winning proposals, client communication, and pricing strategies.
