Course Syllabus

What You Will Learn

Learn the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Automation. Understand how AI integrates with robotics to create intelligent systems.

Explore robotics hardware components, microcontrollers, actuators, and sensors. Learn how robots perceive and interact with the physical environment.

Master Python basics and Robot Operating System (ROS). Learn to program robot movements, control systems, and automation workflows.

Understand supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning for robotics. Train robots to make decisions, improve performance, and adapt to environments.

Use OpenCV, TensorFlow, and AI-driven vision systems. Train robots for object detection, recognition, and navigation through computer vision.

Learn AI-powered automation tools like UiPath and Automation Anywhere. Automate repetitive tasks, workflows, and business processes with intelligent bots.

Connect robots with IoT devices and cloud platforms. Learn AI-driven remote monitoring, control, and predictive maintenance.

Work with collaborative robots, industrial arms, and manufacturing robots. Learn AI-powered automation for real-world industry applications.

Build real-world robotics projects integrating AI automation. Explore the future of humanoid robots, autonomous vehicles, and AI-driven robotics innovations.
